Korea's Four Seasons

  • Spring
  • Summer
  • Fall
  • Winter

Spring

Full Blossom: It is a season that flowers blossom everywhere.
The weather is getting warmer and the daily temperature range difference is very severe.

Summer

Be Colorful : It's a season of the highest temperature and humidity in a year.
Summer makes cities is colorful.

Fall

The fall of the leaf : Leaves change yellow and red, it looks gorgeous.
The beauty of mountains reaches a peak during the fall of the leaf.

Winter

It's the highest cold season.
Winter looks lonely because of cold, but it's a season that many people to share with love.
